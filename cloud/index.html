xNet Cloud

A managed hub that is yours alone

xNet is local-first and self-hostable. xNet Cloud runs your hub for you — dedicated, isolated, backed up, and always reachable — so your data syncs everywhere without you babysitting a server.

What you get

The convenience of managed hosting, without giving up the things local-first is for.

A hub that is yours alone

No shared tenancy. We provision a dedicated, isolated hub per account — encrypted backup, relay, and full-text search, managed and upgraded for you.

Local-first, never locked in

Your data lives on your devices first and syncs to your hub. Move between self-hosted and managed any time — the app never calls home to a control plane.

You hold the keys

Your data identity is a passkey on your device, separate from billing. We hold encrypted bytes we cannot read. Recover your account by email; your data stays yours.

How onboarding works

From "never heard of it" to "syncing across all my devices" in four steps.

  1. 1

    Sign up

    Sign in with WorkOS AuthKit — email, social, or your company SSO. This is your billing identity, recoverable by email.

  2. 2

    Pick a plan

    Choose a tier and check out securely with Stripe. We provision a hub that is yours alone — no shared tenancy.

  3. 3

    Connect your app

    Open xNet on web, desktop, or mobile, create your passkey, and approve a short code to claim your hub. Your data identity stays on your device.

  4. 4

    Own it

    Manage billing, add seats, export everything, or delete your data — from one dashboard. Cancel any time; self-host with the same data whenever you like.
