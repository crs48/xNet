# xNet Maintainers

Maintainers can merge PRs in their area, triage issues, and cut releases. See
[`GOVERNANCE.md`](./GOVERNANCE.md) for how the role works, how it is earned, and
how it grows past the current founder-led (BDFL) stage.

> **Today xNet is a single-maintainer project.** This file is deliberately short
> and honest about that. It becomes a real roster — with per-area ownership and the
> two-maintainer-approval rule — once there are 3+ recurring non-founder
> maintainers (Trigger 1 in `GOVERNANCE.md`).
